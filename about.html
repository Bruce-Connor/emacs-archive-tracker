EAT (emacs-archive-tracker) is a script in emacs-lisp to track some statistics about emacs package archives. It runs every couple of hours and automatically updates this page.
Currently included archives are:

("gnu"       . "http://elpa.gnu.org/packages/")
("marmalade" . "http://marmalade-repo.org/packages/")
("melpa"     . "http://melpa.org/packages/")
("melpa-stable" . "http://stable.melpa.org/packages/")
("user42" . "http://download.tuxfamily.org/user42/elpa/packages/")

The Org-mode repository isn't tracked as it only ever contains 2 packages.

If you know of any other repositories please create an issue and let me know.
