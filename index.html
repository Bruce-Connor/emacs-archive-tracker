EAT (emacs-archive-tracker) is a script in emacs-lisp to track some statistics about emacs package archives. It runs every couple of hours and automatically updates this page.
Currently included archives are:

("gnu" . "http://elpa.gnu.org/packages/")
("marmalade" . "http://marmalade-repo.org/packages/")
("melpa" . "http://melpa.milkbox.net/packages/")
